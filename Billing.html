HOW TO PAY YOUR BILL


Fees for Uninsured Services

Not all services are insured and paid for by OHIP. Click here to download current fees for uninsured services, effective April 1, 2023. These fees may apply to:

  • Ontario residents for uninsured services
  • Canadian residents for uninsured services
  • Non-residents to Canada for services rendered.

Payment Options
PAY ONLINE by CREDIT CARD

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Debit. Use our secure web payment method 24/7 and receive an immediate receipt by email. You will need your patient account # from the upper right-hand corner of your invoice (for example ER012345678).

Click here to pay online

PAY ONLINE through your Financial Institution

Using your Bank website - add "Cambridge Memorial Hospital" as a payee; please include your patient account# (from the upper right hand corner of your invoice - please see sample invoice below) in the description field. Plese note that we cannot accept e-transfers.

PAY BY PHONE using Credit Card

Call 519-621-2333 ext. 2278

PAY BY MAIL - cheque payable to Cambridge Memorial Hospital

Attn. Patient Accounts Cambridge Memorial Hospital 700 Coronation Blvd Cambridge, ON N1R 3G2

PAY IN PERSON - Cash, cheque, VISA, Mastercard, American Express and Debit

Finance/Patient Accounts Wing C, Level 1, Office 241 Monday to Friday, 9am - 4pm; closed weekends and statutory holidays
